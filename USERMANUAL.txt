📱 User Guide – Health Consultation Booking App
This guide walks you through using the platform, whether you’re a patient or a doctor. The app uses your crypto wallet (via Privy) for authentication – you can log in with email, Google, or a wallet like MetaMask.

🟢 For Patients
1. Connect Your Wallet
Visit the website: https://healthconsult-neon.vercel.app

Click “Connect” (or “Connect / Sign Up”) in the top‑right corner.

Choose your login method: Email, Google, or Wallet (MetaMask/Rabby).

If you choose a wallet, make sure you are on the Sepolia testnet.

2. Choose Your Role
After connecting, you’ll be asked: “Are you a patient or a doctor?”

Select “I’m a Patient”.

(Optional) Tick “Remember my choice” so you don’t have to select again next time.

3. Book an Appointment
On the homepage, click “+ New Appointment”.

You’ll see a list of registered doctors. Select one.

Pick an available time slot from the dropdown.

Fill in your name and a description (e.g., symptoms).

Click “Book Appointment”.

Your wallet will pop up – confirm the transaction (a small gas fee on Sepolia).

Wait for the transaction to be mined – you’ll see a success alert.

4. View Your Appointments
After booking, you’ll return to the homepage.

Under “Your Appointments”, you’ll see a list of your bookings.

Each appointment card shows:

Patient name (you), doctor’s short address, date, and status (Pending / Confirmed / Completed).

Click “View Details” to see the full information, including the on‑chain date and status.

5. Check Appointment Status
The status updates automatically:

PENDING – waiting for doctor confirmation.

CONFIRMED – doctor has accepted.

COMPLETED – consultation is done.

You’ll also see a greeting like “Good Morning, [your ENS or wallet address] 👋” when you’re logged in.

6. Change Role or Disconnect
In the navbar, you can switch to Doctor role (if you’re also a doctor) by clicking “Switch Role”.

To log out, click “Disconnect” – your address will disappear from the dashboard.

🔵 For Doctors
1. Connect Your Wallet
Same as patients: connect via Privy (email, Google, or wallet).

2. Choose Your Role
Select “I’m a Doctor”.

If you haven’t registered yet, you’ll be redirected to the Doctor Registration page.

3. Complete Your Profile
Fill in your full name, email, specialty, hospital, location, years of experience, and a short bio.

You can also toggle “Auto-confirm appointments” – if enabled, appointments will be confirmed automatically without your manual approval.

Click “Register” – you’ll be taken to your Doctor Dashboard.

4. Create Availability Slots
In the Doctor Dashboard, scroll to “Manage Slots”.

Pick a date, a start time, and an end time for the slot.

Click “Add Slot”. The slot will appear in the list.

Patients will see these slots when booking appointments.

5. View Pending Appointments
In the Dashboard, the “Appointments” section shows all bookings made with you.

Each card displays:

Patient name, date, description, and current status.

You can also refresh the list using the “Refresh Appointments” button.

6. Confirm, Reject, or Complete Appointments
For a PENDING appointment, you can:

Confirm – accept the appointment (triggers a wallet transaction).

Reject – cancel the appointment (database only, no contract call).

For a CONFIRMED appointment, you can:

Complete – mark the consultation as done (triggers a wallet transaction).

After confirming or completing, the status will update automatically on both your dashboard and the patient’s view.

7. Edit Your Profile
Click “Edit Profile” in the dashboard to update your details (specialty, hospital, etc.).

Changes will reflect immediately in the patient booking form.

8. Manage Slots
You can delete any slot that hasn’t been booked yet – useful if your schedule changes.

9. Switch Role or Disconnect
In the navbar, you can switch back to Patient role if needed.

Click “Disconnect” to log out.

🌟 For Both Roles – Extra Features
Theme & Appearance
Click the gear icon (⚙️) in the navbar to open theme settings.

Choose between Light or Dark mode.

The color palette is fixed to a clean medical theme – no extra themes to confuse.

Health Tips
Below your appointments, you’ll find “💡 Daily Health Tips” – a curated list of wellness advice.

You can filter by category and view all tips on the dedicated /tips page.

Admin Panel (For Admins Only)
If you have admin privileges, you’ll see an “Admin Panel” link at the bottom of the homepage.

There you can manage doctors, hospital settings, and view statistics.

❓ Common Questions
Q: What if I don’t see any doctors?
A: Make sure at least one doctor has registered and created slots. If not, ask a doctor to sign up.

Q: The wallet pop‑up doesn’t appear?
A: Check that your wallet (MetaMask/Rabby) is unlocked, you’re on Sepolia, and pop‑ups are allowed for the site.

Q: I see “Invalid date” on an appointment?
A: This usually means the appointment was created before the date fix was deployed. New appointments will show the correct date. Old ones will display “Not set”.

Q: Can I use the same wallet for both patient and doctor?
A: Yes, but you’ll need to switch roles in the UI. The contract allows the same address to act as both, but for realistic use, separate wallets are recommended.
